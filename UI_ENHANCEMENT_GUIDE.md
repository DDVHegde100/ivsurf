## 💡 Pro Tips for New Users

### Start Here
1. **Paper trade first** - Practice without real money
2. **Understand the Greeks** - They control your risk
3. **Focus on liquid options** - Stick to high-volume stocks
4. **Start small** - Learn with small positions
5. **Education first** - Use our guides extensively

### Common Mistakes to Avoid
- **Ignoring bid-ask spreads** on illiquid options
- **Over-leveraging** with options
- **Not understanding time decay** (theta)
- **Forgetting about volatility risk** (vega)
- **Trading without a plan**
